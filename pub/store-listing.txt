Full description:
This app adds Maritime buses information to MonTransit.

This app provides the buses schedule and news from @MaritimeBus1 on Twitter.

Maritime buses serve Quebec, New Brunswick, Nova Scotia and Prince Edward Island, Atlantic Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by Lock Media.
http://www.lockmedia.com/gtfs/feeds.asp

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-maritime-bus-android

This app is not related with Maritime Bus and Lock Media.

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news from Twitter.
